Help & Instructions

Everything you need to get started with Tool Inventory

Getting Started

1

Add an Item

Click Add Item in the sidebar. Fill in the name, category, and quantity. That's all you need to get started.

2

Assign to a Container

Type a container name (e.g., "Red Toolbox") and location (e.g., "Garage"). If the container doesn't exist yet, it's created automatically.

3

Print a QR Label

Go to Containers, click View QR on any container, then hit Print Label. Stick the printed label on the physical box.

4

Scan to Find

Point your phone camera at a QR label. It opens straight to that container's contents — no app download needed.

How QR Codes Work

Print the Label

Each container gets a unique QR code. Print it from the Containers page.

Stick on the Box

Tape or stick the label directly on the physical container, shelf, or drawer.

Scan to View

Open your phone camera, point it at the QR code, and instantly see what's inside.

Bulk Import via CSV

Already have a spreadsheet? Save it as a .csv file and upload it on the Admin page. The system will create items and containers automatically.
